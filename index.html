CLARIFYING PARTICULARS

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the scheduled possession date for the residences?

Aurelia Residences is slated to receive municipal occupation certificates in Q2 2027, with owner keys handover and personalized interior fit-out inspections commencing in Q3 2027.

Are owners permitted to bring their private interior designers?

Yes. While we offer curated luxury interior designs by renowned global design houses, owners have full freedom to deploy private architectural teams. Our structural and mechanical engineers will cooperate during custom layouts planning.

What is the parking allocation per residence?

Each residence comes with a minimum of 3 gated car park bays. The Sky Villas are allocated 4 bays, and The Grand Penthouse includes 6 private parking bays inside a locked secure parking sub-vault, all pre-wired with high-output EV charging stations.

How is security maintained in the building?

We implement military-grade security systems including biometric facial scanning at elevator portals, 24/7 armed lobby patrols, advanced license plate readers at gates, and isolated service elevators for staff and logistics deliveries.
